# GOAT Battle Arena

## Overview

GOAT Battle Arena is an AI-powered football debate simulator that turns legendary football rivalries into a round-by-round battle.

Choose two football icons and let AI create a fictional face-off based on their real careers, achievements, failures, controversies, and unforgettable moments.

Whether it's Messi vs Ronaldo or Maradona vs Pelé, the goal is simple: analyze the legends, generate the arguments, and decide who wins the ultimate GOAT battle.

## Features

* Select any two football legends for a battle
* AI-generated arguments and roast-style responses
* Uses real career achievements, setbacks, and controversies
* Round-based battle format
* AI-generated winner decision
* Responsive interface for desktop and mobile

## How It Works

1. Pick two players
2. The AI analyzes their football careers
3. Each player gets rounds to challenge the other
4. Arguments are generated using real football history
5. The AI compares both sides and declares a winner
